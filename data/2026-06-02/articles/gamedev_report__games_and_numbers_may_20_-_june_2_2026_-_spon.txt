PC/Console
Star Citizen by Cloud Imperium Games has raised over $1 billion from 6.56 million people
. The game has been in development since 2010, with no full release date announced.
The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt has sold over 65 million copies, per CD PROJEKT’s quarterly report
. A year ago, the figure was 60 million. The Witcher franchise revenue in Q1 2026 grew 36% year-over-year to 44.7 million Polish zloty ($12.3 million). A new DLC was also announced, set to release in 2027.
Per Alinea Analytics estimates, Forza Horizon 6 has sold around 5 million copies across all platforms
in the first couple of days. Total revenue has exceeded $325 million, with approximately 1.7 million players purchasing the early-access premium edition.

Subnautica 2 by Unknown Worlds
sold 4.1 million copies in five days after its Early Access launch
.
According to Alinea Analytics, this is the fastest start among PC releases of 2026
: 1.4x faster than Slay the Spire 2 and 3x faster than Crimson Desert. Including Game Pass subscribers, the audience reached about 6.5 million people, and revenue exceeded $100 million.
Kenshi by Lo-Fi Games has crossed 3 million copies sold eight years after launch
. Two years ago, the game had 2.3 million copies.
007 First Light by IO Interactive sold 1.5 million copies in three days after launch
. Peak CCU on Steam was 68,500. The game has 91% positive reviews on Steam and marks the most commercially successful launch in the studio’s history.
Heroes of Might and Magic: Olden Era from Unfrozen and Hooded Horse has crossed 1 million copies sold in 28 days after its Early Access launch
.
Far Far West by Evil Raptor has sold over 1 million copies in three weeks after its Early Access launch, with 96% positive reviews on Steam
.
REANIMAL from the team behind Little Nightmares has been purchased over 1 million times since its February 2026 release
, per Embracer Group’s annual report.
Paralives by Paralives Studio sold 250,000 copies in 8 hours after its Early Access launch
.
