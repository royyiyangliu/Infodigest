The rising need for FDEs
Since the FDE role originated in the early 2010s at Palantir, it’s become one of the hottest jobs in tech. The original insight was simple: building great enterprise software requires deep customer alignment.
Today, the FDE role has emerged as the critical enabler for agentic platforms.
We’re still uncovering the blueprint for the AI-native enterprise, and the teams closest to customers are the ones drawing it. That same proximity is what makes agentic products successful. Agents are only as sophisticated as the context and workflows they can reach, which demands real immersion in customer operations.
For AI-native products, the work happening in the field right now isn’t just deployment - it’s R&D. FDEs are at the frontlines, engaging with the customer to discover, integrate, and build under tight timelines and with emerging expectations for highly-personalized software.
The best FDEs combine technical depth and customer empathy to navigate complex enterprise orgs, earn stakeholders’ trust, and deliver measurable outcomes.
We’re launching this fellowship to give these FDEs a space to trade stories, pressure-test ideas, and develop the playbooks this moment demands.
We’re looking for fellows who:
Are deploying new AI products and systems into enterprise orgs to make a meaningful, measurable dent in the business
Think in scalable systems, not just individual features
Combine technical depth with product intuition, exceptional communication, and real passion for the craft
You might call yourself an FDE, Applied AI Agent Builder, Director of AI Deployment, or something else entirely. Titles don’t matter - what does is your mindset and approach.
