The number of game development companies in Germany grew from 917 in 2025 to 956 in 2026, an increase of 4%.
The strongest growth came from development studios, up 6% YoY to 474 companies.
Companies that both develop and publish their own projects grew 3% to 427.
The number of publishers grew 2% to 55 companies.
Since the federal game funding program was announced, the number of new companies in the country has grown by 50% over four years. Following the program’s end, the company count fell 4% YoY in 2025.
