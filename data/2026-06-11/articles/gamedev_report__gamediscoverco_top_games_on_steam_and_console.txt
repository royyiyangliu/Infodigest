Top 10 New Releases on Steam by Copies Sold
Subnautica 2 was the May headline launch, selling 4.35 million copies and reaching a CCU of 462,000.
Forza Horizon 6 was purchased by 2.58 million players on Steam alone in May, earning over $152,2 million on the platform. The game shows strong engagement: 165,000 CCU and a median playtime of 23 hours.
Gamble With Your Friends (just over 2 million copies) was the indie hit of the month. Players gamble at a casino with friends, BUT share a single bank account.
007 First Light came in fourth (851,000 copies, with sales starting May 27), followed by the life sim Paralives in fifth (540,000 copies).
Top 10 New Releases on Steam by Revenue
Forza Horizon 6 ($152.2 million) and Subnautica 2 ($105.9 million) were the top-grossing new titles on Steam in May.
❗️New release sales figures include pre-orders.
007 First Light earned $39.9 million on Steam in May. Across all platforms, the game has already sold over 2 million copies and generated over $110 million in revenue in May.
LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight tells a similar story. It earned $19.51 million on Steam (around 350,000 copies), with total revenue across all platforms totaling around $80 million (approximately 300,000 copies on Xbox and over 600,000 on PlayStation).

Top 10 Games on Steam by Copies Sold (B2P)
The original Subnautica rode the sequel launch to 1.34 million copies sold in May. It was available at 75% off and in a bundle with Subnautica 2, and hit a CCU of 51,000 during the sequel’s release window, a new record.
Far Far West sold 925,000 copies in May, bringing total sales to 1.3 million.
Heroes of Might & Magic: Olden Era sold 847,000 copies in May, pushing total sales past 1 million.
Librarian: Tidy Up the Arcane Library! has now sold over 750,000 copies (670,000 in May alone).
Top 10 Games on Steam by Revenue (All Games)
Forza Horizon 6 displaced Counter-Strike 2 ($131.6 million in May) at the top of the revenue chart. GameDiscoverCo notes, however, that estimating CS2’s exact revenue is difficult.
Fourth by revenue in May was Apex Legends ($43.1 million), sixth PUBG: Battlegrounds ($34.85 million), and seventh Diablo IV ($26.11 million), the latter boosted by a new DLC launch.
Console Game Sales
Forza Horizon 6 on Xbox was purchased by an estimated 2.2 million players per GameDiscoverCo. Including Game Pass users, the total number of players exceeded 10 million.
GameDiscoverCo estimates 2.7 million players received Subnautica 2 through Game Pass.
The May PlayStation Plus lineup brought 4.5 million new players to EA Sports FC 26. Wuchang: Fallen Feathers was downloaded by 1 million players, and Nine Sols by 650,000.
Yoshi and the Mysterious Book was the top new release on the eShop (150,000 copies). Metroidvania Mina the Hollower also performed well on the platform, with around 130,000 digital eShop sales and over 350,000 across all platforms.
